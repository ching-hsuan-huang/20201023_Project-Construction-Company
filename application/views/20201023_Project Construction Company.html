WHO WE ARE

WE ARE THE LEADERS IN THE CONSTRUCTION INDUSTRY

Since our incorporation in 2006, we've grown from a regional organization to a multi-faceted, national presence in the industry with headquarters in New York and regional offices in Miami, Washington DC, and Los Angeles.

Our roots are based in construction, real estate, investment and development management. Our portfolio includes work across industries. Project's uniqueness comes from understanding what it is to be the owner, the developer and the builder – a trifecta that is evident in Project's core portfolio of prestigious commercial, corporate and residential properties.

TECHNOLOGIES

BUILDING INFORMATION MODELING (BIM)

Building Information Modeling (BIM) is the process of generating and managing building data throughout the project’s life cycle. It bridges the virtual world with the real world to collect all vital information, enlightening the project team and the project itself. Project is a vocal advocate for the industry adoption of BIM as a revamping of the construction process in addition to being a valuable tool for project efficiencies and cost controls.
